Billing days (credit cards). The first number is the statement close day — the day of month the card's statement cycle closes (same day every month; days 29–31 clamp to the month's last day when needed). It drives statement-date assignment on CSV import and the Reconcile Card wizard. The second is the optional payment due day. Edit either and click Save.
Why is there no Delete? Transactions reference their account forever — deleting an account would orphan its history. Deactivate instead: the account disappears from the import dropdown and new-transaction pickers, but every transaction it already holds keeps rendering everywhere. Reactivate any time.
